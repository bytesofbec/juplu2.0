About Us

Juplu group has been started with an objective to provide healthy and nutrient rich snacks for all age groups and has taken all the efforts to come up with brilliant combination of flavors. Juplu is a delicious and nutritious snack made from the seeds of water Lily plants called -Makhana- which is handpicked by our experts. Makhana has been consumed for centuries all over India and across the world. We have given a traditional Indian touch to this healthy snack with a blend of aromatic Indian spices. It is time to grab a lighter, crispier and tastier bite available in different flavors. "The healthiest, tastiest brings wow In your mind, so, take a taste."
